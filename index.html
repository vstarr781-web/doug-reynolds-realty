Finding Your Why

Real estate isn't just about properties — it's about the people inside them. That's why I start every client relationship by finding your "Why." What's driving this move? What does your ideal life look like? When I understand your story, I can find the right chapter.

I graduated from CSU Sacramento in 2006 with a degree in Real Estate and Land Use Affairs, and I've been mastering the Sacramento market ever since. I consistently sell 25-35 homes per year across Sacramento, Placer, Yolo, and El Dorado Counties, with 600+ total homes closed to date.

I'm recognized as a trusted listing agent, move-up buyer specialist, and College-Glen neighborhood expert — a community I've served since 2009. I assemble a team of trusted professionals — lenders, inspectors, contractors — so every detail is covered. I'm not just your agent. I'm your advocate, your advisor, and your resource for life.
